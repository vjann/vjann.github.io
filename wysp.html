Wendy's Magical Mathcraft (prototype)

Wendy's Magical Mathcraft was developed for and under the guidance of TeachMe TV LLC. Others who worked on it: Skye Kothari, Nathan Sorscher. Math specialist: Jess Soodek.

A pedagogically informed adventure game, designed to familiarize students with math strategies.

Though this game focuses purely on addition and subtraction for K-2 students, it was specially designed to be extendable! Layers of abstraction were created to simplify adding new problem types, math tools, and levels/scripts.
