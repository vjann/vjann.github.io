PhaseBok App For iOS and Android

PhaseBok is an React Native + Firebase end-to-end app designed to provide a simple and friendly platform for the ordering of photobooks. It is currently undergoing testing and the last stages of development. Here are some major features:

  • Intuitive ways to choose photos: directly pick them from your phone and/or share a google photos album directly to the app!
  • Easy payment with ECPayment gateway
  • Easily track your order as it progresses through printing and delivery
